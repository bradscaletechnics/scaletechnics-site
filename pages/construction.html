The Problem

If you run a construction company in Canada, this is your week:

  • Tenders on Merx, BC Bid, and Central Tenders posted while you're on site — you find out three days later when the window is half closed.
  • 20 hours of manual bid prep per job: spec extraction, subcontractor coordination, material pricing cross-checks. Repeat for every submission.
  • WorkSafe BC compliance documentation pulled together by whoever's available — which means whoever's least billable.
  • Subcontractor insurance certificates expiring mid-project. You find out at the worst possible time — when a site inspector shows up.
  • Change orders tracked across email threads. Disputes happen because nobody can find the original scope language from three months ago.
  • Payroll runs late because site timesheets come in three different formats from three different crews and someone has to reconcile them by hand.

Capabilities

The Construction Agent runs your ops layer end to end.

  • 01
    Monitors Merx, BC Bid, and Central Tenders 24/7 — alerts on every matching opportunity within minutes of posting, filtered to your trade category and region.
  • 02
    Pre-populates bid documents from spec sheets using AI extraction — pulls quantities, scope language, and exclusions automatically. You review, not rebuild.
  • 03
    Tracks all WorkSafe BC obligations: safety programs, incident reporting, NCSO certification renewals, and site-specific safety plans — generated and filed automatically.
  • 04
    Manages your full subcontractor database: insurance certificates, WCB clearance letters, expiry dates, and performance history — 30-day alerts before anything lapses.
  • 05
    Logs every change order with timestamp, original scope reference, dollar amount, and client sign-off status — full audit trail, no disputes about what was agreed.
  • 06
    Generates weekly project status reports automatically — progress vs. schedule, open RFIs, upcoming milestones, and subcontractor status delivered every Friday morning.
