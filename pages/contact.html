Common Questions

How long does setup take?

Most systems are fully live within 7–14 days. Discovery and configuration happen in the first week. We test before anything touches a real lead.

Do I need to change my current tools?

Usually not. We integrate with your existing CRM, calendar, and ad platforms where possible. If a tool needs replacing, we handle the transition.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on which agents you need and the scope of integration. We'll give you exact numbers during the walkthrough — no vague quotes.

Is this a SaaS subscription?

No. You're not paying for access to a platform. You're paying for a built and installed system. There's an ongoing optimization retainer, but no per-seat licensing.

What if I'm not technical?

Good. We build everything. You control the system through iMessage, WhatsApp, or SMS — channels you already use. No dashboards to learn.

Can I see it running before committing?

That's what the walkthrough is for. You'll see the actual system live — not slides, not mockups. The real thing, applied to a real business scenario.
