The Challenge

Indigenous civil contractors in BC are leaving government contracts on the table.

  • Federal PSIB set-aside contracts exist specifically for Indigenous businesses — but most companies don't have the bandwidth to monitor, respond to, and win them consistently.
  • ISC, Scale AI, and PSIB funding programs offer millions in non-dilutive capital — but the application process is document-heavy, time-consuming, and constantly changing.
  • WorkSafe BC, traffic control licensing, bonding requirements, and Indigenous procurement certifications all require ongoing compliance management most small teams can't sustain.
  • General contractors and government agencies want Indigenous-led partners — but they need companies that show up with documentation, capability statements, and fast response times that match their process.
  • There's no system tracking which tenders qualify for Indigenous set-asides, which funding programs are open, and which compliance deadlines are coming up — all at once.

Common Questions

PSIB, ISC funding, and how this works.

  • Q
    What is PSIB and how do we qualify? PSIB (Procurement Strategy for Indigenous Business) requires the federal government to award a minimum 5% of eligible contracts to Indigenous-owned businesses. To qualify, your company must be at least 51% Indigenous-owned and registered in Canada's Indigenous Business Directory. Once registered, you can bid on mandatory set-asides worth billions annually.
  • Q
    What ISC funding is available for Indigenous construction companies? ISC offers sequential business capacity funding from $150K up to $1.5M, plus the First Nations Infrastructure Fund for larger infrastructure projects. Scale AI also provides up to 40% reimbursement on AI adoption costs — these can be stacked. ScaleTechnics tracks all open deadlines and pre-fills your applications.
  • Q
    Does ScaleTechnics work with traffic control companies in BC? Yes — our Indigenous consulting practice is built in active partnership with Shewalh, a BC Indigenous-led traffic control company. The AI systems we've developed are proven in live operations, not theoretical. When we install for your firm, we bring tools already tested on real BC contracts.
  • Q
    How does the AI find PSIB-eligible tenders? The agent monitors Buyandsell.gc.ca, BC Bid, Merx, and Central Tenders continuously — filtering every posting by PSIB set-aside status, trade category, and your region. You get an alert within minutes of a matching opportunity. No more checking portals manually or missing windows.
