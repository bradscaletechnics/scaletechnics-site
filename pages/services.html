Your AI Team

Six agents. Every
angle covered.

Each agent is purpose-built for a specific function and connected to every other agent in the system.

01
Agent 01 — Revenue

Sales Agent

Your fastest salesperson. Engages every new lead within 60 seconds — day or night. Qualifies intent, handles objections, and moves prospects through your pipeline without you lifting a finger. Personalized to your voice, trained on your offers.

What it does

  • Responds to every inbound lead within 60 seconds via SMS, email, or chat
  • Qualifies prospects using custom scoring based on your ideal client profile
  • Runs multi-touch follow-up sequences until a response or booking is secured
  • Manages pipeline stages in your CRM automatically
  • Tracks deal velocity and flags stalled opportunities for human review
  • Handles objections with trained, on-brand responses
02
Agent 02 — Media

Ad & Campaign Agent

Manages your advertising intelligently. Monitors performance across platforms, shifts budget toward what's working, writes ad copy, and reports results — all without you logging into a dashboard. Your ad spend works harder around the clock.

What it does

  • Monitors Google, Meta, and other ad platforms continuously
  • Reallocates budget toward top-performing campaigns in real-time
  • Pauses underperforming ads before they drain your budget
  • Generates and rotates ad creative variants for A/B testing
  • Delivers plain-English performance summaries via text
  • Executes budget changes on your command via iMessage or SMS
03
Agent 03 — Calendar

Booking Agent

Fills your calendar without back-and-forth. Books, confirms, reminds, reschedules, and recovers no-shows — fully automated. Your calendar stays full and your no-show rate drops significantly from day one.

What it does

  • Books appointments directly into your calendar in real-time
  • Sends automated confirmations via SMS and email
  • Runs reminder sequences 24h and 1h before every appointment
  • Re-engages no-shows with a same-day recovery sequence
  • Manages waitlists and fills cancellation slots automatically
  • Respects your availability rules and buffer times
04
Agent 04 — Support

Customer Service Agent

Never leaves a customer waiting. Handles inquiries, resolves common issues, and routes complex situations to the right person — instantly, across every channel. Your customers feel taken care of. Your team handles only what actually needs them.

What it does

  • Responds to customer inquiries 24/7 across SMS, email, chat, and social
  • Draws from your knowledge base for accurate, context-aware answers
  • Escalates complex issues to the right team member automatically
  • Monitors customer satisfaction signals and flags at-risk accounts
  • Handles review requests and reputation management post-service
  • Runs reactivation campaigns for lapsed customers
05
Agent 05 — Social

Social Media Agent

Keeps your presence alive without consuming your time. Schedules content, engages with your audience, monitors mentions, and grows your following — continuously, in your brand voice. You stay top of mind without thinking about it.

What it does

  • Schedules and publishes content across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and more
  • Responds to comments and DMs in your brand voice
  • Monitors brand mentions and competitor activity
  • Identifies trending topics and content opportunities
  • Routes inbound social leads to the Sales Agent
  • Reports weekly on engagement, growth, and reach
06
Agent 06 — Growth

Outreach Agent

Builds pipeline while you sleep. Researches ideal prospects, crafts personalized outreach, and runs multi-channel sequences across email and LinkedIn. When a prospect responds, the Sales Agent takes over seamlessly.

What it does

  • Identifies and researches prospects matching your ideal client profile
  • Writes personalized outreach based on prospect data and context
  • Runs multi-touch sequences across email and LinkedIn
  • Manages responses and hands warm leads to the Sales Agent
  • Tracks outreach performance and optimizes messaging
  • Maintains your prospect database clean and current
